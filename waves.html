The Great Wave off Kanagawa

Katsushika Hokusai

Year: 1831

This iconic Japanese woodblock print depicts a towering wave threatening boats near Mount Fuji. It is a masterpiece of the ukiyo-e genre and remains one of the most recognized artworks globally.
