Starry Night

Vincent van Gogh

Year: 1889

"The Starry Night" is one of Vincent van Gogh's most iconic works. Painted from the window of his asylum room, the swirling night sky and bright stars reflect his turbulent emotional state. It has become a symbol of post-impressionist art.
