The Persistence of Memory

Salvador Dalí

Year: 1931

One of Dalí’s most recognizable works, this surreal painting features melting clocks symbolizing the relativity of time. It blends dreamlike visuals with philosophical undertones, making it a cornerstone of the Surrealist movement.
